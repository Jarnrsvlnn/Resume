Reincarnasia

A text-based novel-like interactive story game

Reincarnasia is a narrative-driven action-adventure game where you play as a protagonist who has lost most of their memories, guided by Nelly, a quirky pig companion, through the vibrant yet enigmatic streets of Manila. To recover your fragmented past, you must confront the Apex Predators—powerful embodiments of despair, unfaithfulness, death, and misfortune—each representing a dark chapter of your life. With a branching narrative system, your choices shape the story, determining whether you overcome your inner demons or succumb to the weight of your forgotten past.
